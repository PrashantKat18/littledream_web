MANAGER’S DESK

MANAGER’S MESSAGE

I would like to say that “To read is to learn, to learn is to grow, to grow is life”. There can be no substitute for a best friend like books and no better way of improving your command on a language and expanding your vocabulary. Books are like a ocean where you can seek the treasure of immense knowledge of everything in and around the universe, no gadget can replace book reading.

There are few statements which we need to always keep in our mind:-

  • Education comes from within; you get it by struggle and effort and thought.
  • Education is the key to success in life, and teachers make a lasting impact in the lives of their students.
  • The purpose of education is to replace an empty mind with an open one

My Dear Children,

The school was established in Feb’2016 on occasion of Basant Panchami and perform Maan Saraswati Poojan by Mr. Rakesh Katiyar (Manager) and Mrs. Renu Katiyar (Principal) The school growing day by day by resulting students performance through hard work of Management and staff.
